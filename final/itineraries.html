Sample Family Itineraries

Simple 2-3 day plans to help you make the most of your national park adventure.

Yellowstone

Yellowstone: 3-Day Family Adventure

Day 1: Geysers & Hot Springs

  • Morning: Old Faithful area (2-3 hours)
  • Lunch: Picnic at park
  • Afternoon: Grand Prismatic Spring overlook
  • Evening: Wildlife viewing in Lamar Valley

Day 2: Canyon & Waterfalls

  • Morning: Grand Canyon of Yellowstone
  • Lunch: Canyon Village
  • Afternoon: Easy hikes to waterfalls
  • Evening: Rest at lodging

Day 3: Wildlife & Departure

  • Morning: Hayden Valley wildlife viewing
  • Lunch: Lake area
  • Afternoon: Departure

Grand Canyon

Grand Canyon: 2-Day South Rim Trip

Day 1: Rim Views

  • Morning: Visitor Center & Mather Point
  • Lunch: Grand Canyon Village
  • Afternoon: Rim Trail walk (easy sections)
  • Evening: Sunset at Yavapai Point

Day 2: Desert View Drive

  • Morning: Desert View Watchtower
  • Lunch: Picnic at scenic overlook
  • Afternoon: Junior Ranger program

Great Smoky Mountains

Great Smoky Mountains: 3-Day Family Getaway

Day 1: Cades Cove Loop

  • Morning: Scenic 11-mile loop drive
  • Lunch: Picnic in the cove
  • Afternoon: Easy nature trails and wildlife viewing
  • Evening: Check into Gatlinburg lodging

Day 2: Waterfalls & Trails

  • Morning: Laurel Falls hike (2.6 miles)
  • Lunch: Sugarlands Visitor Center
  • Afternoon: Grotto Falls trail
  • Evening: Explore Gatlinburg

Day 3: Newfound Gap & Departure

  • Morning: Drive to Newfound Gap overlook
  • Lunch: Cherokee, NC
  • Afternoon: Oconaluftee Visitor Center & departure

Glacier

Glacier National Park: 3-Day Mountain Adventure

Day 1: Going-to-the-Sun Road

  • Morning: Start at West Glacier entrance
  • Lunch: Logan Pass Visitor Center
  • Afternoon: Hidden Lake Overlook trail (3 miles)
  • Evening: Many Glacier area lodging

Day 2: Many Glacier Valley

  • Morning: Swiftcurrent Lake easy loop
  • Lunch: Many Glacier Hotel
  • Afternoon: Boat tour or Grinnell Lake trail
  • Evening: Wildlife viewing

Day 3: Lake McDonald & Departure

  • Morning: Lake McDonald scenic area
  • Lunch: Apgar Village
  • Afternoon: Trail of the Cedars boardwalk & departure

Arches

Arches National Park: 2-Day Desert Discovery

Day 1: Iconic Arches

  • Morning: Balanced Rock & Windows Section
  • Lunch: Picnic at Devils Garden
  • Afternoon: Delicate Arch viewpoint (easy) or hike (3 miles)
  • Evening: Sunset at Park Avenue

Day 2: More Exploration

  • Morning: Sand Dune Arch (easy family trail)
  • Lunch: Moab town
  • Afternoon: Fiery Furnace viewpoint & Junior Ranger program
